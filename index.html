About Us

We are an early-stage venture capital firm dedicated to partnering with visionary founders at the beginning of their journey. We are interested in AI first startups in the foundation model, infra as well as the application layer, recognizing that true market change happens when transformative technology meets tangible use cases across diverse domains from education to healthcare, fintech to robotics. We back the audacious innovators, the seasoned domain experts, and the creative problem-solvers who are redefining how business is done, from established sectors to emerging frontiers. With our expanding portfolio of growing companies, we pride ourselves on being more than just capital—we are long-term partners who believe in radical transformation our founders are working on with their vision.


Early Stage Focus

We are dedicated to providing the foundational capital and strategic support at the earliest stages. We are looking for problem solvers and visionary founders working on interesting ideas and important painpoints.


Domain & Sector Agnostic

We look for AI-enabled innovation with a broad domain interest, spanning traditional enterprise solutions and consumer products to foundation models and emerging technologies.


Future Vision

We seek startups with the potential to see and execute a long term vision, shifting paradigms and fundamentally changing their domains.


Founder First Partnership

Our support extends beyond funding. We integrate with our founders to provide whatever is needed, whether it's strategic guidance, operational support, or network access, ensuring we are aligned with their journey to success.

Our Investment Thesis

Our core thesis is built on the belief that enduring value is created by exceptional teams who are leveraging technology to reshape established industries and pioneer new ones. We invest in startups that are poised to deliver a definitive, next-generation solution, seeking out teams led by domain experts and creative problem-solvers who have a clear vision. We hold a long-term perspective for businesses that not only disrupt their immediate market but possess the potential to evolve, adapt, and drive profound, lasting change across their entire domain, specifically targeting companies that can permanently shift how business is transacted and perceived in their industry. We also maintain a high conviction in foundational, emergent technologies, actively seeking out disruptive companies in many high-risk, high-reward sectors, including those advancing applications in robotics, quantum computing and more.


The Domain Experts and Problem Solvers

Teams who have an unparalleled understanding of the problem, and closely familiar with the domains they operate in.


Transformative Applications

Indispensable solutions across a wide variety of domains, from B2B SaaS to consumer platforms, addressing real pain points.


The Visionaries & Paradigm Shifters

Companies that are not just incrementally improving processes, but those that have the potential to permanently shift how business is done in their industry.


Data Moats and Defensible Technologies

Companies that has a plan on how to differentiate themselves from the competition with their product and data.
