About Us

We are an early-stage venture capital firm dedicated to partnering with visionary founders at the beginning of their journey. We are interested in AI first startups in the foundation model, infra as well as the application layer, recognizing that true market change happens when transformative technology meets tangible use cases across diverse domains from education to healthcare, fintech to robotics. We back the audacious innovators, the seasoned domain experts, and the creative problem-solvers who are redefining how business is done, from established sectors to emerging frontiers. With our expanding portfolio of growing companies, we pride ourselves on being more than just capital—we are long-term partners who believe in radical transformation our founders are working on with their vision.


Early Stage Focus

We are dedicated to providing the foundational capital and strategic support at the earliest stages. We are looking for problem solvers and visionary founders working on interesting ideas and important painpoints.


Domain & Sector Agnostic

We look for AI-enabled innovation with a broad domain interest, spanning traditional enterprise solutions and consumer products to foundation models and emerging technologies.


Future Vision

We seek startups with the potential to see and execute a long term vision, shifting paradigms and fundamentally changing their domains.


Founder First Partnership

Our support extends beyond funding. We integrate with our founders to provide whatever is needed, whether it's strategic guidance, operational support, or network access, ensuring we are aligned with their journey to success.
